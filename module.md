# 🚀 ChipCraft Internship Report

## RTL Design, IP Integration, SoC Design & ASIC Physical Design

### 👨‍🎓 Internship Overview

This repository contains the documentation, lab work, and learning outcomes from my **ChipCraft VLSI Design Internship**. The internship focused on the complete ASIC design flow—from **RTL Design** to **GDSII generation**—using industry-standard open-source EDA tools.

The training included hands-on implementation, verification, synthesis, timing analysis, physical design, and RISC-V SoC subsystem development.
